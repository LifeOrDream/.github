# LifeOrDream ⛩️

Infrastructure for **player-owned game economies**.

We build the economic layer that turns on-chain games into **self-sustaining worlds** — programmable tokens with enforced tax policies, adaptive emissions, DNA-driven NFTs that are characters first and assets second, and an AI content engine that turns gameplay into a living show.

## MineBTC — live on Solana

**[minebtc.fun](https://minebtc.fun/)** · A casino-speed country war where 12 nations of HashBeasts race to mine **degenBTC**. Pick a country, call Climb / Hold / Fall, bet SOL — and every minute of play feeds rounds, a country race, NFT progression, and a token economy at once.

- **degenBTC** — Token-2022 with a fixed 2.1B supply (mint authority revoked) and a 0.1% transfer tax routed to burn, country treasury, and mining-vault recycle.
- **Two clocks** — 60-second Casino Rolls resolved by on-chain slot-hash entropy (with a 1-in-625 jackpot), folding into a ~4-hour Country Race that ranks all 12 nations.
- **HashBeasts** — a 36,000-cap genesis run (3,000 per country) of Metaplex Core NFTs with on-chain DNA. They mutate, evolve, breed, rebirth, and boost staking — playable operators, not static art.
- **Dynamic emissions** — snapshot-based price discovery retunes round emissions each cycle (loosens slowly on strength, tightens faster on weakness), with fees funding buybacks and permanent protocol-owned liquidity (LP burns).
- **Country-specific staking** — stake degenBTC or LP, boost with HashBeasts, up to a 9x combined hashpower multiplier.

## Hashiden — the show written by gameplay

**[The open-source studio](https://github.com/LifeOrDream/ai-content-engine)** behind Hashiden (ハシデン): every ~4-hour war cycle becomes a chapter, 42 chapters make a weekly season, and the cast is the HashBeasts players own. The show's compute is funded by the game's own fees — *the fee writes the film*.

- **[ai-content-engine](https://github.com/LifeOrDream/ai-content-engine)** — the creative studio: world canon, chapter writing, ceremonies, NFT asset pipeline, and trailer tooling. Portable to any game world.
